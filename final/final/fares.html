Discount ID Cards

The following ID cards qualify you for discounted fare and passes on most services (Spec-Tran and Shopping Bus excluded):

CATA Student Identification

CATA Student ID cards are available through local middle and high schools at no cost. Showing your CATA Student ID entitles you to pay a discounted 60¢ student fare in Zone 1, $1.25 in Zone 2, or buy a student pass or discount Zone 2 card. All students in grade 6 and above are eligible for a CATA Student ID card. There is a $5.00 fee to replace a card. Call 517.394.1000 for details.

Student Identification Cards

Show a valid ID card from your school enables you to to pay a discounted 60¢ student fare in Zone 1, $1.25 in Zone 2, or buy a student pass or discount Zone 2 card.

CATAClub Card

CATAClub is a free membership program exclusively for CATA customers who have a disability (temporary or permanent) or are 62 years of age or older. This card entitles you to discounted CATA cash fares and bus passes on most CATA services. Discounts do not apply to Spec-Tran or Shopping bus services. There are two types of CATAClub cards. To get a card, you must apply and be approved.

CATAClub Annual

CATAClub Annual cards are issued to persons with a temporary disability. They are valid for a calendar year.

CATAClub Lifetime

CATAClub Lifetime cards are issued to persons with a permanent disability or persons 62+ years. They are valid for up to ten years depending on when you apply. There is a $5.00 fee to replace a lost or stolen CATAClub card.

Medicare Card

Anyone showing a valid Medicare Card qualifies to ride at the discounted rate of 60¢ in Zone 1 or $1.25 in Zone 2, Medicare cards can also be shown to purchase a discount Zone 2 card or a Value Pass.

ADA Card

Persons with disabilities may show their ADA (Americans with Disabilities) Card to ride at the discounted rate of 60¢ in Zone 1, $1.25 in Zone 2 and can purchase a discount Zone 2 card or a Value pass. This applies only to fixed-route service.
